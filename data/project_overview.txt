ProjectMind AI is an AI-powered knowledge copilot for large enterprise projects. It centralizes definitions of data fields, sprint activity, team ownership, and documentation into a single queryable interface.

Objective: Eliminate dependency on subject matter experts by making structured project knowledge directly accessible via natural language.

Teams involved:
- Data Architecture
- Underwriting
- CRM
- Product
- Operations
- Sales
- Claims
- QA
- Auto
- Billing
- Scrum Master

Architecture: A small retrieval layer atop structured JSON mock data. Demonstrations use either an MCP-based AI agent or a standalone RAG pipeline.

Industry context: Insurance transformation project migrating legacy systems to a modern data platform.
